Guidelines

FEES
  1. Besides, Fees paid at the time of admission students shall also pay Annual Fees for 2nd year and final years.
  2. Monthly tuition fees and all other dues must be paid by the 15th of the month failing which a fine of Rs. 10/- per month will be levied.
  3. Fee collection Time : 9:30 AM to 1:00 PM.
  4. All applications for freeship etc. must be from the parents/Guardians of students.
  5. Applications for T.C., Character Certificate, Bonafide Certificate, Appearing Certificate, Stipend holder’s certificate etc. must be made to the Principal. However, it will be issued only on the next day.
  6. Library: Books applied for will be issued between 8 AM & 10 AM on the days specified for each class.
  7. Books must be used with care and must be returned on or before the scheduled date. A fine as fixed by the Librarian will be levied if the book is not returned by the fixed date or is lost or mutilated. All fresh issues will be held up in the meanwhile.
  8. Loss of identity card must be reported immediately in order to check misuse. Application for fresh identity card must be made.
  9. At the end of each academic year, a “No Dues” certificate shall have to be obtained and entries made in the space provided for, from relevant departments such as library/finance etc.
