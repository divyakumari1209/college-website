Physics

General Understanding of Biotechnology, its uses and future prospects

Biotechnology is defined as any technological application that uses biological systems, living organisms or derivatives to make modified products or processes in particular use. It has uses and application in various fields such as agriculture and industries (food, pharmaceutical, chemical, textiles, medicine, nutrition, environment, animal sciences etc.) makes it one of the fastest sectors of growth. Biotechnology utilizes cellular and bio molecular processes to create technologies & products that help improve our lives and the nature. It is used to make life in raw, new products and has the latest technology and applied science.

Psychology

Psychology is the scientific study of the human mind and the reasons for people’s behavior. It’s not about reading minds but a discipline that includes the study of human behavior. It provides a set of tools and theories for understanding how humans think and learn. By studying psychology one could go into teaching, counseling, writing (of course!), human resources, research, and so on. Psychology provides the tools to become a behavioral trainer, which can prove useful for pets, children, and even significant others.
